Problem Statement: Homework

It is difficult for college students to track all of their assignments and homework, which can lead to some students missing due dates and failing assignments.

Team Sketches: Student Organizer App

The team sketches demonstrate all aspects of the application, from the download to the app itself. The app has a number of options to help the student get organized and be on time. Some examples are: Creating a Reminder, Importing a Syllabus, Creating a Schedule, and having access to Blackboard. The app is free to download and helps students in all aspects of school.
