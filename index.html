Problem Statement: Homework

It's difficult for college students to track all of their assignments and homework, which can lead to some students missing due dates and failing assignments

Affinity diagram: Homework Organization

Our affinity list different ways college kids can keep up with their homework and classes. We then listed possible platforms that they could do this.

Team Personas

I am failing all my classes - Sam Smith

Team Storyboards

Sam Smith was failing all his exams and classes so he downloaded the student organizer app, input his due dates, and started to ace everything.

Team Sketches: Student Organizer App

The team sketches demonstrate all aspects of the application, from the download to the app itself. The app has a number of options to help the student get organized and be on time. Some examples are: Creating a Reminder, Importing a Syllabus, Creating a Schedule, and having access to Blackboard. The app is free to download and helps students in all aspects of school.
